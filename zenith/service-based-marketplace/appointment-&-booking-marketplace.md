Appointment & Booking Marketplace
--------------------------------
An **Appointment & Booking Marketplace** is a service-based marketplace model where sellers offer **time-based services** such as consultations, sessions, or scheduled appointments. Customers can book these services by selecting an available date and time directly from the marketplace.

Using the **Marketplace app**, marketplace owners can create a multivendor environment where multiple service providers can register and offer appointment-based services under a single platform.

**Marketplace Overview:**  
[Marketplace Introduction](/zenith/introduction/)

This marketplace model is widely used for businesses that rely on scheduled availability rather than physical product delivery.

Booking Product Featured App for Appointment Marketplaces
---------------------------------------
To enable appointment booking within the marketplace, the **Booking Product** featured app is used. This app integrates seamlessly with the Marketplace app and allows sellers to convert their services into **bookable products**.

With the Booking Product featured app:

- Sellers can create appointment-based products  
- Sellers can define available booking dates and time slots  
- Customers can select their preferred date and time while placing an order  
- Appointments are processed through the standard marketplace order flow  

This ensures a smooth and structured booking experience for both sellers and customers.

Key Benefits of an Appointment-Based Marketplace
--------------------------------
Implementing an appointment and booking marketplace using the Booking Product featured app offers several advantages:

- **Structured Time-Slot Booking**  
  Services are offered through predefined time slots, reducing scheduling conflicts.

- **Reduced Manual Scheduling**  
  Automated booking eliminates the need for manual appointment management.

- **Seller-Level Booking Management**  
  Each seller can manage their own service availability and bookings independently.

- **Seamless Order Integration**  
  Appointments are handled as marketplace orders, keeping the workflow consistent and centralized.



Why Use Booking Product for Appointment Marketplaces
-------------------------------
Appointment-based marketplaces require a reliable system to manage time-based services. The **Booking Product featured app** fulfills this requirement by enabling sellers to offer bookable services while maintaining full integration with the Marketplace app.

This setup allows marketplace owners to scale service-based offerings without additional customization or external booking systems.
