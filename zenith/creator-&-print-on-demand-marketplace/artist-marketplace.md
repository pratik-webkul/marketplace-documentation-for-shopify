Artist Marketplace
---------------------------
An **Artist Marketplace** is a creator-focused marketplace where artists can upload their designs or artworks and sell them as products through a multivendor platform.

Using the **Marketplace app**, marketplace owners can onboard multiple artists and allow them to manage their own designs and products while selling under a single marketplace.

**Marketplace Overview:**  
[Marketplace Introduction](/zenith/introduction/)

Artist marketplaces are ideal for platforms that focus on creative content and design-driven products.

-
Common Use Cases for Artist Marketplaces
---------------------
An artist marketplace can support various creative business models, such as:

- Artwork and illustration marketplaces  
- Graphic designer marketplaces  
- Independent artist selling platforms  
- Creator-driven merchandise marketplaces  

This model allows artists to focus on creativity while the marketplace handles product listing and order flow.


Artist Product Design Featured App
--------------------
To enable artist-specific workflows, the Marketplace app provides the **Artist Product Design** featured app. This app allows admins to create and manage artists and enables artists to upload their designs within the marketplace.

With the Artist Product Design featured app:

- Admin can create and manage artists  
- Artists can upload designs or artworks  
- Designs can be associated with marketplace products  
- Products created from designs can be sold through the marketplace  

This makes it easier to manage a structured artist ecosystem within the marketplace.

How Artist Product Design Supports an Artist Marketplace
--------------------
- Centralized artist management by admin  
- Design-based product creation  
- Artist-specific product workflows  
- Seamless integration with marketplace orders  



Why Use Artist Product Design for Artist Marketplaces
-------------------------
Artist-focused marketplaces require a dedicated structure to manage creators and their designs. The **Artist Product Design** featured app fulfills this requirement by enabling artist onboarding and design-based product selling within the Marketplace app.

This allows marketplace owners to scale creative marketplaces without custom development.
