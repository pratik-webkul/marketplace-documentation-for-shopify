Print-on-Demand Marketplace
------------------------------
A **Print-on-Demand Marketplace** is a marketplace model where products are created and printed only after an order is placed. This eliminates the need for inventory and allows sellers to offer customizable products.

Using the **Marketplace app**, marketplace owners can build a multivendor print-on-demand marketplace where sellers offer printed products such as apparel, accessories, and merchandise.

**Marketplace Overview:**  
[Marketplace Introduction](/zenith/introduction/)

Print-on-demand marketplaces are ideal for businesses looking to sell customized or branded products without maintaining stock.

Common Use Cases for Print-on-Demand Marketplaces
---------------------------
Print-on-demand marketplaces are commonly used for:

- Custom apparel marketplaces  
- Merchandise selling platforms  
- Personalized product marketplaces  
- Brand merchandise stores  

This model reduces operational overhead while enabling flexible product creation.

Print-on-Demand Products & Dynamic Mockups
---------------------
The **Print-on-Demand Products & Dynamic Mockups** feature allows marketplace owners to offer products with dynamic previews and on-demand printing.

With this functionality:

- Products are created dynamically  
- Designs are previewed using mockups  
- Printing is triggered only after purchase  
- Orders can be fulfilled using print services like Printful  

This provides a smooth experience for both sellers and customers.


How Print-on-Demand Supports a Marketplace
-------------------
- No inventory management required  
- Dynamic product visualization using mockups  
- Order-based printing and fulfillment  
- Seamless integration with marketplace product flow  



Why Use Print-on-Demand for Marketplaces
---------------------------
Print-on-demand marketplaces allow sellers to offer a wide range of products without upfront inventory costs. By combining print-on-demand capabilities with the Marketplace app, marketplace owners can scale product offerings efficiently.

This model is especially suitable for creator-driven and customization-focused marketplaces.
