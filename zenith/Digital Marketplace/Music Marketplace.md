## What is a Digital Music Marketplace?

A digital music marketplace is an online platform where users can explore, purchase, and download music-related digital content.

This includes:
- Songs and albums  
- Vocals and sound effects  
- Instrumentals and beats  

Once purchased, users can access their music anytime on their devices without physical delivery.
