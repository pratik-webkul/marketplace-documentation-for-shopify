Now allow your Sellers to add subscription for their products and provide maximum customer retention on your store.

Allow your sellers to create their own Subscription on a weekly, monthly, or yearly basis.

To get this feature simply enable the Product subscription feature app in your Multivendor Marketplace app.

Note: To use this feature it is mandatory to enable the Stripe connect feature app in the Multivendor app. It is also compatible with Make an offer products

Currently, it only works with stripe payment but soon we are going to launch this feature app with the Shopify payment method too and update it constantly.

So please keep checking this blog for more updates coming in this feature app.

Also please make sure that you have set the Payment Flow as "Payment on Same page" in Stripe connect feature

Activation

To enable this app, visit the "feature apps" section of the Multivendor Marketplace for Shopify.

Now Activate the Product Subscription App from the feature app section. After that, accept the charges and you are ready to use the app.

Admin End

Once you enable the app, A new submenu will be visible in Multivendor Admin Panel.

You need to go to the Admin Panel >> Configuration >> Subscription Product Configuration

On the redirected page you will find the details that the admin can manage for the Seller subscription pack and products.

Add Subscription

Sellers can only add subscriptions variant-wise for the products. So they can add the subscription while creating or editing their product variant.

Hence sellers will have the option to enable it while creating/editing variants of a product as shown below:

After saving the details Seller can enable/disable the plan anytime for that product.

View Subscription

Seller can view their Subscription for more details by Clicking on Three dots in the Action section as shown below

On the redirected page they will get all the details and they can also get order details particular product variant by Clicking on view orders as shown below.

Order details

Front-End

To show the Subscription product widget on your front end you need to add some codes in your Shopify store.

Admin will find the instructions and Codes by going to Admin Panel > Configuration > Instruction for Marketplace.

In case you find any difficulty in adding the codes then please contact us at support@webkul.com and we will assist you in adding the codes to your store.
